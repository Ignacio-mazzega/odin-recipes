Quick Chicken

Chicken, broccoli, and stuffing casserole.

    Ingredients

  • 3 cups prepared stuffing
  • 2 cups cubed, cooked chicken
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar chicken gravy
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

    Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
  2. Mix stuffing, chicken, broccoli, chicken gravy, and half the Cheddar cheese in a large bowl. Spread into the prepared baking pan; sprinkle with remaining Cheddar cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through and Cheddar cheese is melted, 20 to 25 minutes.
